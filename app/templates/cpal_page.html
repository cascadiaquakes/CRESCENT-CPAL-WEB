About

CPAL Paleoseismic Compilation

The Paleoseismology Group at Cascadia Quakes studies past earthquake events through geological evidence — ancient fault lines, sediment layers, and other markers — to improve our understanding of seismic hazards in the Cascadia Subduction Zone.

The map displays the USGS and CPAL's latest compilation of paleoseismic proxies, represented by distinct symbols and colors. Click any marker to explore its associated dataset.

Map Controls

  • Dataset & Plot Controls — Toggle the filter panel to refine the display.
  • Right-click near a marker cluster — Spreads overlapping markers apart; right-click again to regroup.
  • View Home — Resets zoom and viewport to the default.
  • Clear Highlight — Removes highlights from previously highlighted markers.
  • Group Markers — Returns spread markers to their original positions.
  • Spread Offset — Adjusts the distance between spread-out markers.
  • Select a Site — Zooms to a chosen site from the list.
